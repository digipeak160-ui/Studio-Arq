About the Architect

Combining rigorous architectural thinking with a passion for craft.

Architect — Jane Doe, M.Arch

Jane is a licensed architect and designer with 12+ years of experience in residential and commercial architecture. Educated at the University of Arts, her approach is rooted in careful site analysis, sustainable practices, and a restrained palette that prioritizes light and materiality.

Her studio focuses on creating humane spaces that are elegant, durable, and deeply connected to context. She leads design from concept through construction documentation, collaborating closely with craftsmen and consultants to realize every detail.

Education: M.Arch, University of Arts. Experience: 12 years across adaptive reuse, bespoke residences, and hospitality projects.
